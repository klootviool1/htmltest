Mercedes-Benz AMG Project ONE

It is finally here, the all new Mercedes-Benz Hypercar! With Hamilton's sucessfull 1.6 V6 engine, this machine is little less,
F1-car. This car is made with Formula One tech from the ground up. It is said to produce more than a thousand horsepowers,
accelerate from 0-200 km/h in less than 5 seconds and will have a top speed of 350 km/h!

It is said that this car is a massive slap in the face of Mclaren. For instance, the car is called The Project One.
What is Mclaren's hypercar called? The Mclaren P1. The P1 produces above 900 bhp, where the new Mercedes will produce
more than a 1000.

This rivalry goes way back however, because since the F1 season of 2015, Mercedes stopped producing their engines for Mclaren.
Mclaren have since then received engines from Honda and those turned out to be unreliable. Since then Mercedes-Benz
has really opened a large gap between themselves and Mclaren.
